The Facial Region

Video loop, 3m46s

Ghost Pattern Waves, Gallery 400, Chicago, IL, 2017

My Data Double uses self-portraiture to unpack the data residue left behind by a internet-connected media device user and means to resist data extraction.

Pieces include: Sleep a spinning sculpture that appears a blur to the naked eye but still through a frame-rate-synchronized live camera feed, Motion Sickness a zoetrope with a face vomiting in an infinite loop, and The Facial Region a video loop using a camera rig that moves according to facial tracking.
