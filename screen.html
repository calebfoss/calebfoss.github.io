Screen


2016,   4min26sec,   Performance with Kinect and custom app
Dance by Jacob Buerger
Sound recording/design by Michael Hart

Screen is a performance recorded via Kinect, with imagery generated from depth readings and sounds cued by body tracking. With a packet of data functioning like a film frame, continuous data extraction acts as its own form of cinema.
